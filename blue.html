How it works

Our platform collects data from sensors, robots, apis and satellites

We train our ML-model to learn the patterns, the estructure and the XRay of the marine ecosystem.

This data allows us to analyse the XRay of their a ecosystem.

After quantifying their data, our marketplace allows our customers to sell contracts of marine resources to market consumers and/or producers.

Data scrapping & analysis


1 / We fetch and consume ocean data from a multitude of sources, including but not limited to IOT devices, sensors, APIS, satellites, NASA data api platform, worldbank sources and from IPM1 - our own proprietary hardware model.

2 / All these data is passed through our plataform to get them in a common format that eases storing, analysis, and further processing. A process mostly known as ETL

Machine Learning & Model Training


After processing and storing sensor and api data, we feed them to our ML/AI models for training and accuracy. The model searches for patterns, common structures and non-linear relationships between sensor-data and ecosystem activity, size and behaviour.

Smart Resources Marketplace


After all data crunching and learning we provide our customers accurate reports of marine resources and an XRay of marine-resources which they can sell on our marketplace through smart-contracts that guarantee efficient resource management.
